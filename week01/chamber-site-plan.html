Overview

Site Name: Boise Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city (Boise) in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Boise Chamber of Commerce is an organization that encourages growth and operation for the various businesses in the Boise area. Visitors to the website will find places to go and things to do, while business owners can learn more about how to improve their business and have opportunities to have their services displayed on the website

Scenarios

  • What information will the chamber provide to guide the operation of businesses in a beneficial manner?
  • Where can I find places to eat while visiting Boise?
  • What are the untapped markets in Boise that I could potentially fill with my business ideas?
